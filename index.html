    About One4One, One4One is an online platform dedicated to fostering inclusivity and empowerment for people with disabilities (PWDs) and ethnic minorities. Our platform serves as a bridge, connecting PWDs and the public through the awareness content and the promoting of locally crafted clothing madeby PWDs and the fabric sources from ethnic minorities.
